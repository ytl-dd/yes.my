  1. What is the NUTP Free SIM?
    The NUTP Free SIM is a Service Plan provided under the Prepaid Service by YTL Communications Sdn Bhd (“YES”). It is available only for the members registered under the National Union Of The Teaching Profession Malaysia (“NUTP”).  Terms and conditions apply.
  2. How do I know that I am eligible for this programme?

    You need to be a member registered under the National Union Of The Teaching Profession Malaysia (“NUTP”). If you believe you qualify for registration, please visit http://www.nutp.org.my/ for member registration. In addition, you do not have more than five prepaid service accounts with YES.

  3. Where can I apply for this programme?
    You can apply for the NUTP Free SIM through our MyYes app or the YES Online Store.
  4. How many NUTP Free SIM Card(s) (“SIM Card”) can I apply for?
    Each eligible subscriber can only apply for one SIM Card.
  5. I have submitted my application for a SIM Card, now what?
    Upon receiving your application, we will verify if you are eligible (see FAQ no.2 for the eligibility criteria) and we will send you an email informing whether you are successful. If your application is successful, your SIM Card will be delivered to the address you provided in the application form.  
  6. I have successfully applied for a free SIM Card and received my confirmation email, how do I know when I will receive it?
    You should receive your delivery tracking number with your successful application email. To check your delivery status, go to www.yes.my/TrackOrder and enter your delivery tracking number.
  7. I have received my free SIM, how do I activate it to start using my free 40GB data right away?  
    To activate your SIM Card, 
    (i) Download the MyYes app
    (ii) On the login page, press “New User”
    (iii) Press “Activate SIM” button and follow the self-activation steps on the screen
    (iv) Once your activation is successful, you can now login to your account using your phone number and the password sent to your email. You can also login using the TAC method if you can’t find your password.
     
  8. Am I allowed to choose a YES Mobile Number when I apply for the SIM Card?
    Not at this point in time. A YES Mobile Number will be assigned to each SIM Card upon activation.
  9. Why do I need to provide my myKad information and perform facial recognition during activation?
    In accordance with the guidelines set by the government, we are required to confirm that all subscribers are genuine and there is no fraud involved. 
  10. How long will my NUTP Free SIM account be active from the date of activation of the SIM Card?
    Your NUTP Free SIM service account will remain active for a period of 60 days from the date of activation of the SIM Card.
  11. What happens if my NUTP Free SIM account becomes inactive?

    You will still be able to receive calls & SMS, access your contacts and make emergency calls. However, you will not be able to make any other calls, send SMS or surf the internet. Kindly purchase a YES Reload for your NUTP Free SIM. The minimum purchase of a Yes Reload is RM5 which has a Validity Period of ten (10) days.
     

    Any unutilised data after the expiry of your account shall be forfeited. 

  12. Does the 40GB free data have an expiry date?
    Yes, it does. It is valid for a period of 60 days only from the date of successful activation of your NUTP Free SIM Card.
  13. How will I know whether I have received my free 40GB data upon activation of my NUTP Free SIM Card?
    Login to MyYes app and the data should be reflected on your home screen.
  14. Can I purchase other Prepaid Data Add-Ons before my account expires?
    Yes, you can. For more information on the Prepaid Data Add-on, please check out our Prepaid FAQ.
  15. Will there be any charges imposed when I call other YES numbers?
    There will be no charges imposed when you call YES numbers from your YES SIM. However, charges will be imposed on all international (IDD) calls and SMS in accordance with the voice calls and SMS rates as more specially published in www.yes.my/roaming.
  16. Will there be any charges imposed when I call other networks?
    Yes, all domestic calls to other networks are subjected to a standard rate. Please refer to the table below:-
     
    Type of Off Net Services Rates (RM) Charging Block (in seconds)
    Voice Calls RM 0.09/minute 30
    SMS RM 0.09/SMS -

     
  17. Will there be any charges imposed on IDD Service?
    Yes, charges will be imposed. Please refer to the voice call and SMS rates in relation to IDD Service @ www.yes.my/roaming.
  18. What if I have more questions not addressed in this FAQ?

    You can send an email to yescare@yes.my or get the MyYes app and manage your account anywhere with our Selfcare app.

  1. What is the Kasi Ong, Kasi Up Promotion?
    Kasi Ong, Kasi Up Promotion is a time-limited promotion launched by YTL Communications Sdn Bhd (“YTLC”) by giving Free 8GB LTE data to both the referrer and referee for every successful referral when the referee activates the new SIM card (“Promotion”).
  2. How long is the Kasi Ong, Kasi Up Promotion period?
    The Promotion will commence on 1st February 2021, 12:00 a.m. until 26 February 2021 11:59 p.m. (“Promotion Period”).
  3. Who is Eligible for this promotion?
    The Promotion is open to all individuals participating in the YES Kasi Up Refer & Earn Programme during the Promotion Period (“Eligible Subscribers”).
  4. How can I redeem the Free 8GB LTE Data?
    The Free 8GB LTE data will be automatically credited into both referrer and referee’s service account, processing will take up to 3 days upon referee successfully activates the new SIM Card. The Free 8GB LTE Data can be used for 30 days from the date the referee successfully activates the new SIM Card (“Free Data Validity Period”)
  5. What happens if there is balance unutilised data from the Free 8GB LTE Data after the Free Data Validity Period?
    Unutilised internet data will be automatically forfeited and will not be carried forward to any of the succeeding days after the end of the Free Data Validity Period.
  6. What type of internet services can an Eligible Subscriber use under the Free 8GB LTE Data?
    An Eligible Subscriber may use the Free of 8GB LTE Data for internet services such as web surfing, video streaming and messaging.
  7. Is an Eligible Subscriber allowed to use the Free 8GB LTE Data for tethering or hotspot?
    Yes, an Eligible Subscriber is allowed to use the Free 8GB LTE Data for tethering or hotspot.
  8. I have signed up for a YES plan that comes with Unlimited Data. How does the Free 8GB 4G LTE Data apply to me?
    If you have subscribed to any YES unlimited data plan, your data capacity is already unlimited. There will be no “add-on” since there is no limit to your data entitlement.
    However, if you purchased the Kasi Up Unlimited Add-On or Kasi Up Unlimited_OKU Add-On, you can use the Free 8GB LTE Data upon expiry of those Add-Ons as long as your service account remains active. For clarity, an active service account means your network service has not been suspended for outbound calls.
  9. Can I purchase YES Data Add-On(s) if I have fully utilised the Free additional 8GB LTE Data?
    Yes, you can purchase YES Data Add-On(s) from the MyYes app. Here's how:

  1. What is Data Raya Campaign?
    Data Raya Campaign is a campaign allowing family members, relatives and friends to purchase and give Data Raya Add-On to Recipient during the Hari Raya Aidilfitri celebration.
  2. How long is the Data Raya Campaign period?
    The Data Raya Campaign is from 17th May 2020, 12:00 a.m. until 20th June 2020, 11:59 p.m.
  3. Who can purchase the Data Raya Add-On?
    Any person can purchase the Data Raya Add-On (“Sender”) by following the steps stated in question 5 below.
  4. Who is eligible to receive the Date Raya Add-On?
    A YES Subscriber with a valid and active Postpaid or Prepaid Service account of a Service Plan, other than those listed below, may receive the Data Raya Add-On from the Sender:
    (a) 1Bestarinet 2GB Teachers Data Plan;
    (b) 1Bestarinet Service Plan;
    (c) Fixed Wireless Internet Plan;
    (d) FIZ 2 Service Plans;
    (e) Yes Speedzy 2 Service Plans;
    (f) Yes 4G Home 2 Service Plan;
    (g) Konfem Unlimited 49/79/99 Postpaid Plan;
    (h) Konfem Unlimited 79/99 Family Plan;
    (i) Konfem Unlimited Supplementary Plan 30; and
    (j) YTLC’s enterprise customers.
  5. How can I purchase the Data Raya Add-On?
    You can purchase the Data Raya Add-On by following the steps stated below:
    (a) login to MyYes4G app (if the Sender is a YES Subscriber) or visit the YESExpress Add-On page available @     https://www.yes.my/ (applicable to both Yes Subscriber and non YES Subscriber);
    (b) key in the Recipient’s YES mobile number or YES ID or Postpaid or Prepaid account number;
    (c) select a Data Raya Add-On that the Sender would like to purchase and give as a gift to the Recipient;
    (d) key in Sender’s name and mobile number respectively; and
    (e) click on the tab “Proceed” and pay through either credit or debit card or by way of online banking transaction.
  6. What are the types of Data Raya Add-On rates and service offerings?
    Please login to MyYes4G app (if the Sender is a YES Subscriber) or visit the YESExpress Add-On page available @ www.yes.my (applicable to both Yes Subscriber and non YES Subscriber) for more information on the types of Data Raya Add-On rates and service offerings.
  7. What is the validity period of the Postpaid Data Raya Add-On?
    The validity period of each Postpaid Data Raya Add-On is thirty (30) days from the date of successful purchase by the Sender (“Postpaid Data Raya Validity Period”).
  8. Will the Postpaid Data Raya Add-On purchased during a particular Billing Cycle expire automatically at the expiry of such Billing Cycle?
    As long as the Postpaid Data Raya Add-On is:
    (1) within the Postpaid Data Raya Validity Period; and
    (2) the Recipient’s Postpaid Account Validity Period is valid and active,
    the Recipient may enjoy and use the allocated internet data in the Postpaid Data Raya Add-On throughout the Postpaid Data Raya Validity Period irrespective of the Recipient’s applicable Billing Cycle. For example, if a Recipient’s Billing Cycle ends on 25th May 2020 and he/she receives a Data Raya Add-On with a validity period of thirty (30) days on 23rd May 2020, provided always that the Recipient’s Postpaid Service account is valid and active throughout the Postpaid Data Raya Add-On Validity Period, he/she will continue to enjoy the internet data in such Postpaid Data Raya Add-On until 21st June 2020. Any internet data which remains unutilized after the Postpaid Data Raya Validity Period will expire automatically after 21st June 2020.
  9. What is the validity period of the Prepaid Data Raya Add-On?
    Each Prepaid Data Raya Add-On has a particular validity period from the date of successful purchase by the Sender (“Prepaid Data Raya Validity Period”). Please refer to MyYes4G app (if the Sender is a YES Subscriber) or visit the YESExpress Add-On page available @ www.yes.my (applicable to both Yes Subscriber and non YES Subscriber) for further information on the Prepaid Data Raya Validity Period.
  10. Which Account Validity Period will apply in the event a Sender purchases multiple Prepaid Data Raya Add-On?
    In the event a Sender purchases multiple Prepaid Data Raya Add-On, the Recipient’s Account Vailidity Period will be extended accordingly based on the last Prepaid Data Raya Add-On’s successful purchase date. For example, if a Recipient’s Account Validity Period ends on 30 April 2020, and on 25th April 2020 he/she receives a Prepaid Data Raya Add-On with a validity period of thirty (30) days and again on 28th April 2020, the Recipient receives another Prepaid Data Raya Add-On with a validity period of thirty (30) days, in such instance, his/her account validity period will be extended to 27 May 2020 based on the Prepaid Data Raya Add-On received by him on 28th April 2020. For clarity and avoidance of doubt, if multiple Prepaid Data Raya Add-On is purchased, the order of priority in calculating the usage of internet data is from the earliest to the latest purchase of the Prepaid Data Raya Add-On.
  11. How many Data Raya Add-On can a Sender purchase at any one time?
    There is no limitation on the number of Data Raya Add-On. A Sender may purchase for use of the Recipient which means a Sender may make multiple purchases of the Data Raya Add-On provided always that the purchase is done by the Sender during the Data Raya Campaign Period.
  12. Will a Sender receive notification upon successful purchase of the Data Raya Add-On?
    A transaction successful page will pop up if the purchase of the Data Raya Add-On by the Sender is successful. The Sender is advised to refer to his/her credit or online banking statement transaction to view details on such purchase of Data Raya Add-On.
  13. Can a Sender request for refund upon successful purchase of theData Raya Add-On?
    There will be no refund initiated by YTLC on any successful purchase of the Data Raya Add-On.
  14. Will a Recipient receive notification if a Sender purchases the Data Raya Add-On for them?
    Recipient will receive notification via SMS or e-mail on the Data Raya Add-On gifted to him/her. Recipient may check further details on the Data Raya Add-On via the “Purchase History” tab on MyYes4G app or MyAccount.
  15. What happens if the Sender entered the wrong YES Active Subscriber mobile number or YES ID or Account Number for Data Raya Campaign?
    It is the responsibility of the Sender to ensure accuracy of the information provided to YTLC in relation to the purchase of the Data Raya Add-On. YTLC assumes no responsibility and/or liability on any wrong or mistaken information provided by the Sender and YTLC shall not be liable for any loss, claim and/or expense incurred by the Sender or Recipient arising from any inaccuracies in the information provided by the Sender.
  16. Who shall I contact if I need more details on the Data Raya Campaign?
    You may email to yescare@yes.my.
